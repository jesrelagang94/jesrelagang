## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Do I need to implement everything at once?**
A: No. You can implement in phases. Content first (Phase 1), then visuals (Phase 2).

**Q: Can I modify the content to match my style?**
A: Absolutely. The provided content is a template. Adjust to your voice and preferences.

**Q: What if I don't have all the skills listed?**
A: Adjust service offerings and skill levels to accurately reflect your actual expertise.

**Q: Do I need professional images?**
A: High-quality stock photos are acceptable. Professional screenshots of your work are better.

**Q: How long will this really take?**
A: Core implementation: 8-14 hours. With testing and refinement: 15-20 hours total.

**Q: What if something breaks?**
A: Use your backup to rollback. Check the troubleshooting section in QUICK-START-CHECKLIST.md.

**Q: Should I add the 5th service (Virtual Assistant)?**
A: Only if you actually offer those services. Four strong services are better than five weak ones.
