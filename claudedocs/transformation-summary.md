## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Challenges

**Challenge 1: Brand confusion during transition**
- Risk: Existing clients expect designer, see developer
- Mitigation: Personal communication to existing clients explaining evolution
- Timeline: Pre-launch announcement email

**Challenge 2: Visual inconsistency**
- Risk: New icons don't match design aesthetic
- Mitigation: Maintain consistent style guide, test thoroughly
- Timeline: Visual assets testing phase

**Challenge 3: SEO ranking disruption**
- Risk: Changed content affects existing rankings
- Mitigation: Maintain URL structure, use redirects if needed, gradual rollout
- Timeline: Monitor for 30 days post-launch

**Challenge 4: Impostor syndrome/self-doubt**
- Risk: Questioning if qualified to claim "multi-discipline expert"
- Mitigation: Document real projects/experience in each area, client testimonials
- Action: Prepare case studies before launch
